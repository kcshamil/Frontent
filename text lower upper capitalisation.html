Without getting too political about the faux pas, isn’t it very strange that the Canadian Prime Minister referred to Diljit Dosanjh as a Punjabi singer? This sectarian label being applied on an international podium is weird and inappropriate, to say the least. Our beloved singer Lata Mangeshkar sang on many distinguished global platforms. No one ever referred to her on stage as a ‘Marathi’ singer.
