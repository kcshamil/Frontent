PUNE/MUMBAI: Maharashtra Pradesh Congress chief Nana Patole on Tuesday alleged that the son of a legislator was in the Porsche Taycan when it crashed into a motorcycle killing two techies on May 19. "An MLA's son was also involved in the case. After the accident, two persons got down from the car and govt should reveal who the other person was," Patole said, while demanding a CBI probe into the case. Patole alleged that Dr Ajay Taware of Sassoon Hospital, "From offwho was arrested Monday for allegedly swapping the blood samples of the minor accused in the case, had links with a politician. Earlier too, Patole said, five-star facilities were provided to drug lord Lalit Patil in the same hospital.icial records, it has been established that a local legislator and a cabinet member played a crucial role in the appointment of Dr Taware (as medical superintendent of the hospital). People of Maharashtra must know the name of the legislator and the minister who recommended Taware's name," he said, referring to NCP MLA Sunil Tingre. On Monday, Maharashtra medical education minister Hasan Mushrif had told TOI that he had received a recommendation from MLA Sunil Tingre's office on Dec 26, 2023, recommending Dr Ajay Taware for the post of superintendent of Sassoon. Later, Dr Taware was removed as superintendent after a controversy involving a rat bite death. "We have reasons to believe that all-out efforts have been made to save the builder's son owing to his links with leading politicians," he said.
